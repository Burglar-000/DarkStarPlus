What is Darkstar?

"In astrophysics, a 'dark star' is a theoretical object that may have existed in the early universe. Unlike conventional stars that shine due to nuclear fusion, dark stars are hypothesized to be powered by the annihilation of dark matter."

"These theoretical stars, fueled by the interaction of ordinary matter with dark matter, shine brightly despite their invisible core. They represent a powerful force hidden within the cosmos."

"Similarly, individuals possess untapped potential, hidden talents, and unexplored depths of thought. Like a dark star, we may not always be aware of the immense power and brilliance that resides within us."
